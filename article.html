An Evening in Paris

Written By Ananya Gupta July 12, 2020
Stories

Overlooking the Seine river, the famous Lovre Museum takes an entire day to look around only a half of the entire museum. The art collection is just amazing & fantabulous.

Moving from Louvre, the next place to watch the spectacular sunset over Paris was from Montparnasse Tower. The famous Eiffel Tower is in the frame of the view from the topmost floor of the tower.

Paris is related with romance, Louvre, Moulin Rouge & many things more. Making friends and drinking coffee alongwith pastries were awesome..
